Amnesty Internationnal, 2024

This animation tells the story of Rita Karassartova, a human rights militant from Kyrgyzstan defending access to water.

With another student, we wanted to show how experimenting with the use of paint can highlight Rita Karassartova's cause. The paint allowed us to create a moving background alike to water.
