Environmental Amnesia, 2025

Environmental Amnesia is my diploma project about the environnmental changes around us that one struggles to see. Especially this project is about the disapearance of the Pyrenean glaciers. I choose to represent it with different digital techniques, such as illustration, interractive display and photomontage. The main purpose was always to show what we have been missing and what will be missed.

Software : After Effects, Figma, Photoshop, Touchdesigner
