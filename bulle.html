Bubbles, 2025

For this projects we had to create a multisense experience in 4 days from scratch, inspired by snoezelen environnment to be used by austitic persons. The main element of our installation is the creation of bubbles, with a buddle machine as well as some home made lava lamps. The interraction had to be a central point, to do so we used buttons to trigger light and sound around the lava lamps, and a pedal on the ground which started the bubble machine.
